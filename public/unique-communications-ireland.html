Client: Tidy Storage

"Bin Chaos vs. Tidy Storage"

[Opening shot: A grey, rainy day in a Dublin neighborhood] [Camera zooms in on a house with overflowing bins in front] [Wind blows the lid open. Trash spills across the driveway. A soggy pizza box flops into a puddle.] [Sound: Wind, rain, squelch, distant dog barking]

[Cut to neighbor's house next door: sleek, modern bin storage units from Tidy Storage] [The bins are neatly hidden. Rain rolls off the roof. Everything looks tidy and calm.] [Close ups of security features]

"Even your bins deserve better. Tidy Storage" [Text on screen / VoiceOver]: "Even your bins deserve better. Tidy Storage" [End with TidyStorage.ie logo over a few quick shots of each size option being assembled]
